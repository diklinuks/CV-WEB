Floating Waste Detection

Computer-vision pipeline for identifying floating waste in surface water from drone footage.

  • Python

The problem

Surface waste in canals, harbours, and lakes is a hard problem to monitor at scale. Drone footage gives wide coverage, but someone has to actually watch the footage or write a tool that does. This pipeline does the second part.

What it does

An object-detection model (YOLO-family) is trained to identify floating waste objects — bottles, plastic fragments, larger debris — in still frames sampled from drone footage. Detected objects are tagged with class, confidence, and frame timestamp, then aggregated across the footage for reporting.

Architecture

Frames are sampled from drone footage at a configurable rate, preprocessed (resize, color normalization), passed through the detection model, and the resulting detections are written out as structured records for downstream reporting.
